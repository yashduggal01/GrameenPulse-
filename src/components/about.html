About GraminPulse+

GraminPulse+ is a smart rural development platform designed to empower rural communities through technology. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life in rural areas by providing essential services and resources that promote civic engagement, safety, and health.

Our Vision

We envision a connected rural ecosystem where every individual has access to the tools and information they need to thrive. By leveraging technology, we aim to bridge the gap between rural communities and essential services.

Our Services

  • Gramin Samadhan: A platform for reporting civic issues and tracking resolutions.
  • Suraksha Seva: Safety services including emergency response and incident reporting.
  • Swasthya Seva: A healthcare hub providing access to medical services and health resources.
